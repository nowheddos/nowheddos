I'm usually very busy with school, so I can't work on projects as much as I'd like.

You may want to know... Why the name "nowhed"? Short answer: I dunno. Long answer: I came up with it in 2019 or so, when I wanted a new Minecraft username. I went to one of those word combiner websites, typed in a bunch of random words (that I do not remember) and it spat out nowhed. What I wanted in a username was one that was meaningless, genderless, and short. I liked it, so it's been my online alias since then. It might have been 'nowhere' and 'dead' or 'head.'

I hope you enjoy viewing my page!
